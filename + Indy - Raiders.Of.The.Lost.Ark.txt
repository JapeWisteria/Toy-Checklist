Adventures of Indiana Jones in Raiders of the Lost Ark
Kenner. 1982-83. 1:18 Scale (3.75").
Waves: 2. Figures: 11.
-----------------------------------------------
All figures have solid construction. Indiana Jones in desert disguise and Monkey man were released exclusively in playsets. Several figures had action features.

The full title of this toy line was “Adventures of Indiana Jones in Raiders of the Lost Ark”.
