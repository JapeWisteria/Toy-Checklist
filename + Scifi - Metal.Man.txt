Metal-Man
Zee Toys/Zylmex. 1976-80. 1:18 Scale (3.75").
Waves: 5. Figures: 7.
-----------------------------------------------
All figures are die-cast and riveted o-ring construction.

These figures were released in many different forms, each coming with unique accessories. To this end, only the figures are listed in a single wave.

The figures were either human or robots. I've listed humans first, then the robots.

Humans
~~~~~~
__ Corporal Chrome
__ Major Mercury
__ Rescueman
__ Sergeant Silver

Robots
~~~~~~
__ Questar
__ Radon
__ Roton
