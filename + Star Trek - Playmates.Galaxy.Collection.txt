Star Trek Galaxy Collection
Playmates. 2009. 1:18 Scale (3.75").
Waves: 1. Figures: 19.
-----------------------------------------------
This line was based off the 2009 film, which re-cast the original crew. All figures came with a piece of the Enterprise that fit together into playsets.

All figures are solid construction.
